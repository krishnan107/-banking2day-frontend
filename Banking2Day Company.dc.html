Banking, explained without the jargon

HOW WE EARN MONEY

Free for you. Honest about who pays us.

Researching on Banking2Day is completely free. We keep the lights on through referral fees — and we're upfront about exactly how that works.

1 · Referral fees

When you choose to continue to a provider and complete a qualifying action, that provider may pay us a fee. It costs you nothing extra.

2 · Always disclosed

Where a paid relationship exists, we show a disclosure near the comparison. You always know before you click.

3 · Facts stay fixed

A fee never buys a better rating, a higher position, or a hidden cost. Rates, fees and limitations are reported as they are.

Our promise

If being paid ever conflicted with showing you the most accurate picture, the accurate picture wins. That principle is the whole reason people trust us.
