1. Introduction

Banking2Day ("we", "us", "our") is an independent information and comparison platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Banking2Day, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information we collect

We aim to collect as little as possible. Depending on how you use the site, this may include:

  • Details you provide — such as your name, email or phone number when you submit an enquiry, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us.
  • Usage information — pages visited, calculator inputs you choose to run, and general device/browser information.
  • Cookies — small files that help the site function and help us understand usage (see section 5).

You can browse, compare and use our calculators without creating an account or sharing personal details.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide the comparison or information you requested.
  • To send the newsletter or updates you have opted in to (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • To operate, maintain and improve the website and our tools.
  • To detect, prevent and address fraud, abuse or security issues.
  • To comply with applicable legal and regulatory obligations.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only: with regulated providers when you choose to proceed with an enquiry; with trusted service providers who help us run the site (under confidentiality obligations); or where required by law. Any referral relationship that may apply is disclosed near the relevant comparison.

5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working, remember your preferences and understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some may affect how parts of the site function.

6. Data security & retention

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw consent or unsubscribe at any time. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email privacy@banking2day.com or write to Banking2Day, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai 400051. We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
