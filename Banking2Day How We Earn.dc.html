HOW WE EARN MONEY

Free for you. Honest about who pays us.

Researching on Banking2Day is completely free. We keep the lights on through referral fees — and we're upfront about exactly how that works.

1 · Referral fees

When you choose to continue to a provider and complete a qualifying action, that provider may pay us a fee. It costs you nothing extra, ever.

2 · Always disclosed

Where a paid relationship exists, we show a clear disclosure near the comparison. You always know before you click.

3 · Facts stay fixed

A fee never buys a better rating, a higher position, or a hidden cost. Rates, fees and limitations are reported as they are.
