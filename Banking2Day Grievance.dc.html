1. Our commitment

At Banking2Day we are committed to providing a fair, transparent and responsive experience. If something hasn't met your expectations, we want to hear about it and put it right. This page explains how to raise a concern and how we handle it.

2. How to raise a complaint

Email grievance@banking2day.com with the following so we can resolve it quickly:

  • Your name and preferred contact details.
  • A clear description of the issue and when it occurred.
  • Any reference number, screenshots or supporting details.

3. Escalation levels

Level 1 — Support team

Write to hello@banking2day.com. Most queries are resolved here.

Level 2 — Grievance Officer

If unresolved within the Level 1 timeline, escalate to our Grievance Officer (details below).

Level 3 — Provider / regulator

For complaints about a specific financial product, the regulated provider and the relevant ombudsman are the final authority (see section 5).

4. Our timelines

  • Acknowledgement — within 48 working hours of receiving your complaint.
  • Resolution — we aim to resolve within 7–10 working days, depending on complexity.
  • Updates — if more time is needed, we'll keep you informed of progress.

5. Complaints about a provider's product

Banking2Day is an independent comparison platform and does not lend, approve or service financial products. For issues relating to a specific loan, card or deposit — such as charges, servicing or approval — please raise them directly with the regulated provider through their grievance process, and, if needed, the relevant banking ombudsman. We're happy to help point you in the right direction.
